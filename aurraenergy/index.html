About STEED Next-Gen

STEED is the new generation Lithium-ion battery brand introduced by **Aurra Energy** to meet the growing demand for highly efficient, reliable, and maintenance-free energy solutions in the UAE and Middle East markets.

Manufactured with advanced lithium technology and intelligent Battery Management Systems (BMS), STEED batteries deliver superior performance, faster charging, and a significantly longer cycle life compared to conventional lead-acid batteries.

  • Longer Runtime
  • Fast Charging
  • Maintenance-Free
  • Smart BMS Protection
STEED ST-Li-24105

Built for GCC Extreme Heat

The harsh climate of the Middle East demands durable energy systems. STEED batteries are developed with deep-cycle active cells and premium thermal barriers to deliver stable, degradation-free performance in high-temperature ambient conditions.

Smart BMS Protection

An integrated intelligent Battery Management System continuously monitors cells for maximum safety. Fully protects against overcharging, deep discharge, short circuit, and overheating.

High Operational Uptime

Features extremely low self-discharge and high energy-density cells. Supporting rapid opportunity charging, these units allow multi-shift material handling operations without battery swapping.
